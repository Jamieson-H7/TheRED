What is TheRED?

TheRED is an online learning platform made by students, for students, of students. Courses here can be perused at one's convenience, as well as uploaded and maintained by teachers and prospective tutors. Teach everyone about anything and everything! Come aboard the Ruby E-Institute of Dreams to make your own education-related fantasies come true!

There are a few things you can do on our site: for example, make an account, learn, or even discuss topics that are related with what you are learning!

Thank you for choosing TheRED!

How does this work?

  1. You log in or sign up

  3. You select one of the courses to learn from
